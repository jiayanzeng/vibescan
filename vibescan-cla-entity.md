This Entity Contributor License Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between
**Yanzeng Jia** (the "Project Owner"), maintainer of vibescan (the "Project"), and the
entity identified in the signature block below (the "Entity"). It documents the rights
the Entity grants in Contributions made by the Entity and by employees or agents of the
Entity designated by the Entity.

If your employees make Contributions to the Project as part of their work, or your
employment or contractor agreements assign their work product to the Entity, the Entity
should execute this Agreement so that those Contributions can be included in every
edition of the Project, including commercially licensed editions.

## 1. Definitions

**"Entity"** means the legal entity signing this Agreement and all entities that
control, are controlled by, or are under common control with it, where "control" means
(a) the power to direct the management of such entity, (b) ownership of more than fifty
percent of the outstanding shares, or (c) beneficial ownership of such entity.

**"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship, including modifications or
additions to existing work, that is intentionally submitted by the Entity or by a
Designated Employee to the Project Owner for inclusion in, or documentation of, the
Project, excluding work conspicuously marked as "Not a Contribution."

**"Designated Employee"** means an employee, contractor, or agent of the Entity whom the
Entity authorizes to submit Contributions on its behalf, as listed in Schedule A or
otherwise designated by the Entity in writing.

Subject to the terms of this Agreement, the Entity grants to the Project Owner and to
recipients of software distributed by the Project Owner a **perpetual, worldwide,
non-exclusive, no-charge, royalty-free, irrevocable copyright license**, **with the
right to sublicense** through multiple tiers, to reproduce, prepare derivative works of,
publicly display, publicly perform, modify, and distribute the Entity's Contributions
and such derivative works.

**Dual-licensing / relicensing.** The Entity expressly acknowledges and agrees that the
Project Owner may license and sublicense the Entity's Contributions, and any derivative
works, under **any license terms of the Project Owner's choosing**, including both the
PolyForm Noncommercial License and separate **proprietary or commercial license terms**.
The Entity retains all ownership of its copyright in its Contributions; this Agreement
grants a license, not an assignment.

Subject to the terms of this Agreement, the Entity grants to the Project Owner and to
recipients of software distributed by the Project Owner a **perpetual, worldwide,
non-exclusive, no-charge, royalty-free, irrevocable** (except as stated in this section)

Project terminate as of the date the litigation is filed.

## 4. Entity representations

The Entity represents that:

1. It is legally entitled to grant the above licenses, and each Designated Employee is
   authorized to submit Contributions on the Entity's behalf.
2. Each Contribution is either the original work of a Designated Employee or work the
   Entity otherwise has the right to submit, and any third-party material is identified
   with its source and license restrictions of which the Entity is aware.
3. The Entity will notify the Project Owner if a person ceases to be authorized to
   contribute on the Entity's behalf, and will keep its list of Designated Employees
   current.
